The Parody of Colonialism and Fascism in Helldivers 2

"You came. You took things that were not yours, and you did not even, for appearances' sake, ask first."

-Jamaica Kincaid in "A Small Place"

Do you like spreading freedom? Do you like forcibly spreading freedom? If so then this game is for you! This is a game about forcing your will on unwilling planets in the name of democracy. Even the first couple sentences in the linked trailer has enough satirical rhetoric to make anyone understand satire. The voice lines and overall theme of the game are very on the nose about being a parody of colonialism and fascist. While you should not take the overall theme at face value, you can take the gameplay itself at face value. The core gameplay loop is shooting bugs and robots and completing various missions to level up and get stronger gear. It's a game that has enjoyable gameplay but is an impeccable work of satire if you stop to absorb the environment that the developers have put you in.
