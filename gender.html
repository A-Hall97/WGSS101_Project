How Celeste Illustrates The Personal Struggle Of Gender Identity

"Before we begin our journey, let's start with you. Who are you? The answers to that all-important question are what make up our identity.

-Blair Imani in "Read This to Get Smarter"

Celeste is a game about Madeline, a young woman who decides to climb Celeste Mountain. What begins as a journey of self-determination, it turns into a narrative about mental-health, self-discovery, and acceptance. Along the way she encounters a mirror image of herself named "Badeline" who seems to be a representation of her self-doubt. It's a beautiful introspective narrative with challenging gameplay to match the challenging theme of self-acceptance.
