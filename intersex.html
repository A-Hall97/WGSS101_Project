Baldur's Gate 3 is one of the most inclusive games in recent times. There is a wide array of representation in this game from the way they allow you to customize your character, to the people you meet along your journey. The focal point of the game is on the actions of the player, instead of who you are as the player. The inclusive approach of the Baldur's Gate 3 developers has expanded to the modding community as well. Many of the large modding sites have outright banned mods that replace characteristics that bigots don't agree with. While you can't argue with these morons, you can prevent them from spreading their hate. It's been really refreshing everyone from the developers, to the community of this game since it's launch come together to ensure the inclusivity of this game is unhindered. Keeping the focus off of the genitals and acknowledge that these people exist is great to see.
